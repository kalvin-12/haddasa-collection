Our Story

Haddasa Collection began with a vision to redefine modesty with elegance.

Established in Jakarta, we have been providing high-quality modest wear that combines traditional values with modern design. Every piece in our collection is crafted with attention to detail and a commitment to quality.

Quality

We use only the finest materials for our collections, ensuring comfort and durability.

Passion

Our designs are born from a passion for modest fashion and empowering women.

Sustainability

We are committed to ethical production and sustainable practices in our supply chain.

Our Mission

To provide elegant and high-quality modest wear that empowers women to express their identity with confidence and grace.

Our Vision

To become a leading modest fashion brand recognized globally for its timeless designs and commitment to excellence.
